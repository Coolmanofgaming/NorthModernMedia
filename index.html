FAQ

Questions We Always Get

How fast can you set up my digital presence?▾
We typically go live within 72 business hours of your onboarding call. More complex builds may take 3–5 days, but we always give you a clear timeline upfront.
Do I own my website and social accounts?▾
100%. Everything we build is yours. Your domain, your website files, your social accounts — you have full admin access from day one. We don't hold anything hostage.
What's the difference between the setup fee and monthly plans?▾
The setup fee is a one-time charge to build your digital foundation. Monthly plans add ongoing management: regular posting, content creation, updates, and growth strategy. Monthly plan setup fee is discounted from $399 to $299.
Can I cancel my monthly plan anytime?▾
Yes — no long-term contracts. You can cancel at the end of any billing period. We ask for 30 days notice so we can wrap up any active work cleanly.
Do you work with businesses in any industry?▾
Yes, we work with all types of local businesses — salons, restaurants, contractors, retail shops, fitness studios, medical offices, auto shops, and more.
What do you need from me to get started?▾
Not much. After your onboarding call, we'll collect some basic business info, any existing logos or photos, and access credentials we need. Most clients spend less than 30 minutes on their end.
How does content creation work on monthly plans?▾
We create professionally designed graphics, captions, and posts based on your brand and industry. Each month, you receive a content calendar for review and approval before anything goes live.
